Projects

Boba Tier

Boba Tier is a full-stack app that lets boba tea cravers rate drinks from their favorite boba tea shops. It also displays the top-rated drinks by city, so boba drinkers can decide which drinks to try out. Users can track the drinks they have rated, and they can also leave comments on the boba tea shop pages. With no app dedicated to finding the best boba tea in town, this app will fill that void.

Technology Tools:

  • React.js
  • Node.js
  • Express.js
  • PostgreSQL
  • HTML/CSS
